My Story

Hi there! I'm Skye Energy Gibbs, and I spent 8 years as a competitive gymnast. I trained at Sugar Land Gymnastics, then moved to World Champions Centre under Coach Cecile and Coach Laurent (Simone Biles' coaches), competing through Level 8 and training up to Level 9. I know what it takes to succeed in this sport, and I also know what it costs.

I walked away from competitive gymnastics before completing Level 9. It wasn't about talent or work ethic. It was about not having the mental support I needed, and not even knowing how to ask for it...
